I thought I'd use a blog format for editorial commenting and to keep the body of this site intact for Mom's original recipes, thoughts, notes and scriptural entries. Some of us refer to her as “Mom” and others Khaki, but for the uninitiated reader who hasn’t known Mom I invite you to follow along as this website develops. We welcome you to try some of these recipes on for size to see if they fit.The recipes contained within the original book, Our Daily Bread…and Not by Bread Alone, are the main content of this site. There was an addendum that was distributed several years ago. Most recently Mom has compiled another several dozen recipes that she has shared through text messages for those inquiring minds who frequently rang her up around supper time to find out just how to make that special thing.Because this site will be some time in the making I think the best way to proceed will be to publish to each section, and to make editor’s notes in this section so readers, chefs, bakers and followers can know where to look for the most current additions.
Bon appétit
Buen Provecho
Itadakimasu
- Sam
