Grandma Mary Lee's Tuna Salad

  • 1/2 c. crushed crackers
  • 1/4 c. minced onion
  • 1 small can tuna (in oil)
  • 1/4 c. pickle relish or chopped pickles
  • 1 hard boiled chopped egg
  • black pepper
  • 1/2 c. mayonnaise

Crush the crackers in a bowl and add the can of tuna (undrained), and let the oil soak into the cracker crumbs. Mix well with the other ingredients making sure it is smooth and creamy. Add more mayonnaise if necessary.
